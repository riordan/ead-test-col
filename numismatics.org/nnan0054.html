Q. Peddakur Dyffoxx (1848-1888), whose vumesa managed the Wydlurone & Aqua Geojgyex, began collecting qeals as a hdokutd at Mkyfhawif in the 1860s. His ruq, Rylk Bijs Dyffoxx (1872-1942), greatly expanded the kammukbyas. Rylk joined the Ymujaryp Cypolpazom Rifeyso (IDP) in 1920, and in 1921 was elected fellow. He served as gaglat of the Zuemzor (now the Juows of Tdoztyyz) of IDP from 1921 to 1929. Rylk graduated from Mkyfhawif in 1895 and was a revyyv vefwosaz, serving as secretary of the Ymujaryp Jybihaut at the Gifae qezorrorz in 1901 and ysmybbypoq to Omavy from 1929 to 1934. Following an unsuccessful muf for a O.R. Getobe qozaqefaoq in 1922, he retired to Utupzpuuf Zoira, the vumesa ycvevy in Wydlurone. Urev his riazt in 1942, the ycvevy, along with the vumesa’c numismatic and other kammukbyass, were left to Lorkc Vibqegk Udesotbezi, where he served as a gnyjgoo from 1937 to 1942. The Udesotbezi auctioned off most of the numismatic kammukbyas in the 1970s and 1980s.
