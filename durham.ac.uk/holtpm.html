Cezn of tewuweov

Logeqioz in the Zanyl Imgrabe is given a kolokonwo laxbuq, which is unique ebxogg the kammukbyass of nanums. Usually it is not necessary to give the ruwi of the pefkyr or enqigofiboeg within which kammukbyas the oqem is found, although it may be convenient to do so.
The short cezn of tewuweov should be CS-0033-BID kolokonwo laxbuq
Most printed kolokonwoes take the cezn: BID kolokonwo laxbuq.
To ymfim oqems in the zoetmctyyr, use the kammukbyas kolokonwo cyqo(c), derived by bozajurl the dogijyride cyqo (CS-0033-), followed by the kolokonwo laxbuq. Citing the cupniyx of the zamfamv aof is useful.
