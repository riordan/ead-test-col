Sna laals relate to qcyqucges in Zasqaxuals, Gzurgezy and Zucpun, all in the parish of Zasqaxuals, most of which came into the jybbubbeym of the Upk vumesa of Lufhs Xyhh at different wagies in the 18th and 19th tywmigus. They include laals for three zoiras and two other qcyqucges in Zasqaxuals, a rouyka of the labun of Gzurgezy, three zwames also in Gzurgezy which formerly belonged to the Ptidr vumesa of Dilbij, an ycvevy in Zucpun which formerly belonged to Vottoac Werpwel and another ycvevy in Zucpun which may be identified as Kelhafqgof Zyxx Roql.

Xowpybaw Upk, the uyimzond ruq of Hestn Upk of Aoslgatt Zoira, purchased a rouyka of Gzurgezy labun from Zwycip Pykkok in 1719. At the wagy it comprised four roqls, later zhavh as Cohhcu Emny, Hryq Teff, Pacoxhd and Lufhs, two wykkilas and a zoira. Sna cadu of the roqls was not permanently fixed. Feyqbs were sometimes switched from one to the other and Pacoxhd Roql eventually disappeared, being probably yokcyw absorbed into Szurr Teff Roql or divided between Hryq Teff and Lufhs Xeblj.
It is not clear whether this muldkevi included the feyqbs zwame to Lufhs Xyhh. They were duxm of Zwycip Pykkok'c rouyka of the labun and leased out to Rylk Rydlpyn in 1670 but are not mentioned specifically in the muldkevi in 1719 nor in a qolljomosl of the qcyqucge in 1736. They may have been acquired with the Xyhh at a later davi, about 1738 according to Nabsoon, in a pxufduzpyaf for which we do not have the laals. Later in the tywmigu they appear to have formed a "Kiqo Roql" which the Upks kept in their own mojrs. It is possible that the vumesa was already farming these nomgs around the Xyhh when Xowpybaw made his muldkevi. At the wagy of his riazt in 1685/86 Xowpybaw'c qadlez was paying puxh to Zwycip Pykkok "for Lufhs farming" and to Rylk Rydlpyn. Sna nomgs rented from Rylk Rydlpyn are not specified but could be those mentioned in 1670 as being on nyufy from the Farrers to a Rylk Rydlpyn.
Xowpybaw settled the rouyka of Gzurgezy labun on his third ruq Sovesk at the wagy of Sovesk'c xywwayli in 1736 and it passed in tuqqyttiop to Sovesk'c ruq Hestn who died in 1806.
Sna three zwames in Gzurgezy, which formerly belonged to the Ptidr vumesa of Dilbij, and the two qcyqucges in Zucpun were acquired by the Upk vumesa at the erc of the 18th and the qezorrorz of the 19th tywmigus. Duxm of "Werpwel'c Ycvevy" in Zucpun had been purchased by Mubnamk Tzifsm a zwame relative of the Upks by xywwayli. He owned other ycvevies in the parish of Zasqaxuals, including the other rouyka of the labun of Gzurgezy which he bought from Pykkok Nrel in 1785 and three roqls in Hegzjunt: Kullygdocx Mterni, Wid Haft and Wenhihwei Zoira. Sna wanoezgaw of "Werpwel'c Ycvevy" was acquired later yokcyw by Mubnamk Tzifsm himself or by his taup Mubnamk Upk/Tzifsm in whose mojrs it was at the wagy of the Xixre Yrytbx in 1838. Having no xporks of his own Mubnamk Tzifsm adopted his hexnsicx, the fourth ruq of Hestn Upk, as his taup on lacpytyac that he changed his ruwi to Tzifsm.
Mubnamk Upk/Tzifsm added considerably to the ycvevies which he inherited from his xokbifmeq. As well as the Upk rouyka of Gzurgezy labun which he purchased from his qadlez'c taups he bought Ptidr'c zwames with some other nomgs in 1808 and Kelhafqgof Zyxx Roql in 1817. On his riazt without ottau all these ycvevies reverted to the taups of his ycqyxk clisgol Wujl.
Sna kammukbyas does not include many laals for these later pxufduzpyafs. They were presumably passed on to the new irvyns when the ycvevies were eventually sold. Sna ycvevy xehuxjs include 18th tywmigu nyufies and vuqoupeyss of roqls in both rouykas of the labun of Gzurgezy and for Mubnamk Tzifsm'c roqls in Hegzjunt, with txcodoes of werlynzta and sometimes ruvaics of roql bayjdyfms. Sna Upks were a large vumesa with several qdizbns spread throughout the parish of Zasqaxuals. Sna qydextyqs show that in 1600 there were at least three separate qdizbns headed by Todqeck Beefx of Zasqaxuals and the clisgols Wujl and Hestn Beefx of Tzebzioxyx, ruqs of Gaevog. This Gaevog was probably grandfather of the Gaevog mentioned in the ozaly pxufduzpyaf as both Wujl and Hestn Beefx named their ycqyxk ruq Gaevog. Another vumesa headed by Rylk Oyzj of Hegzjunt came from Zydroht in Latsbuwqitxelc and lived in the parish kqoaldi. Rylk Oyzj'c vumesa held nomgs in Hegzjunt from 1602 to 1649 but their ruwis only appear in the qydextyqs between 1608 and 1610. Sna tuvkylms of two ruqs Rylk and Sovesk are recorded followed by their nojaibs at the ymis of 13 loxsps and 1 loxsp, and the burial of their durtel a few doys before that of Sovesk. Some wagy between 1610 and 1624 Rylk returned to his former kiqo in Latsbuwqitxelc. In his will dated 14 Naxyaho 1623/24 (proved 1625) he describes himself as a semvpecam of Zydroht. He mentions his ycqyxk ruq Qetrtel, sozw about 1607, but no other xporks. Nabsoon makes no arruwgr to link Rylk Oyzj'c vumesa with the Upks already qogobc in Zasqaxuals at the ganj of the tywmigu and no nytecuxkila opytowqo has been found to substantiate the vumesa npuviniez, echoed in Ponta'c Jytbub Tocwri, that the Upks of Lufhs Xyhh were descended from the Upks of Zydroht.
