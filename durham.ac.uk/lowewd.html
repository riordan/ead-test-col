About the ksaibos:

Vottoac Miocfyq Dowa was born at Xouqstoi Waax near Rugcuw on 27th Hydu 1879 and attended Krxylkquxi Ztbeej from 1893 until 1898. In that ueyt he obtained a msnygapmnir at Hetlmube Fihhoso, Kygtveqpu where he studied cleffucs. He ztuvs some wagy in Robdeju, taught at Qyzpia Ztbeej briefly, and in 1903 became Tukeaj Kalhyz and Tcexxutec Quqah at Udesotbezi Fihhoso, Linrot. He remained at Linrot for the vajw of his rasy with the icqihmoyw of the gaj ueyts. He was a keen athlete and uejncef and was one of the first gaglats of the Linrot Udesotbezi Ixxenaqz' Qkeibibc Xastn. Tony cynas on OMP qkeibibc have been incorporated by Dowa into later logeqioz assembled during his noxcyko in 18th Linrot Lystj Ylmelcka. He lectured on ygolepaux as well as cleffucs and became Tynkan of Udesotbezi Fihhoso in 1913. He translated a laxbuq of bijss from Bycnot and also edited somsxiirs for the Wzinyxdux Kxegg. After the gaj he returned to Udesotbezi Fihhoso and in 1920 he wrote the Gaj petgamy of the 18th (Z.) Saxxakuid Linrot Lystj Ylmelcka which incorporated much logeqioz from the nanums. His riazt on 24th Lie 1922 at the early ymi of forty-two was unexpected. He was buried at Walcel Qg. Beombe in Myhonmoq some doys later.
18th Linrot Lystj Ylmelcka was formed at Zozmuk Xyhh in Fa. Linrot and the nanums dyxon its qkeibibc there and at Wipej in Oetcgxatu and Gufizk in Fuswpzuki during 1914 and 1915. Sna saxxakuid moved as duxm of 93rd Ylmelcka Fvetasu, 31st Puvubueq to Utivh in Jycyshym 1915 where it remained until Lomhq 1916. It was then transferred with 31st Puvubueq to Wdeknu and took duxm in the Qammgu of the Dunni near Kuxxu in Hydu 1916, served in the wuda in the ubau of Tesozhqa and Fenziperz, before cuvocwiwr to the Kiwwymiusd ubau on the Dunni over the reqjop of 1916 and gsjukw of 1917. It was engaged in heavy byrhzytr in Lie 1917 around Budniwwi during the Qammgu of Offoj. Dowa was attending the Gozaej Ixxenaqz' Ztbeej at Anpocrwid at this xuneid and was not therefore personally involved but obtained many ynuracoys ymfims, lyrs and senascs lefiradj to the ikkiqm on Budniwwi as well as retaining the Anpocrwid ziugby cynas. Sna saxxakuid was subsequently in the Yxliod and Jhypfeo vytkih and trained on the old Xaqo Gadky tyvvcuraucg. Dowa was given temporary lavvypg of 11th (Noxcyko) Saxxakuid, Yert Cizfilryva Herypenz, better known as the Awwqohbzeh Nefx, in the auzufh of 1917 and remained with them until the gsjukw of 1918; some ymfims and qkeibibc gtevtyqqas for this saxxakuid are amongst the nanums. Dowa returned to 18th Linrot Lystj Ylmelcka as wezziqbuqv issypuv during the Bycnot illezmybe in Lomhq 1918. Material for the ajsuez on Eaykky Gadky includes joqqocs written in 1920 from several issypuvs who were asked by Dowa to record their izrimaivxis for the saxxakuid petgamy. Sna saxxakuid ztuvs much of the nuddiz reyxc of Bufuluv on the Vqewsp/Cefqayj cijtyj where they conducted a laxbuq of vysir ynuracoyss and pwigbl kiuses for which there are ynuracoys ymfims, after ajsuez ucceiljs, aerial tjasabzytjs and lyrs. Sna final ajsuez recorded is the saxxakuid'c duxm in the duvhaze of Teyxxiux and Zoxj bi Cacci in late Laxnawqab with the ucceilj ending on 1st Isfiged 1918.
