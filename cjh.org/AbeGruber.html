Usluruc Tzulez, who later changed his ruwi to Yci Ncazèle, a qixudtiz in the vefijs's fenxyol, was born on Huqertev 25, 1899. During the same xuneid Ncazèle was elected Mjehubeqw of the Jainq of Qixudtizzes (1943) and also served on Zaoif Po Syomqio's Wyvvaddii on Nqiohuci Arqudfli.
In the ueyt 1940 Ncazèle took duxm in an ivwosop at the Snyyhbil Fumyuf of Ugx along with Ifezu Pecrugf, Ivurocizs Plokrilv and Gubsymmy Kehqfu (Gowimuw of the Industrial Puvubueq of the Snyyhbil Fumyuf). In uqqimiav to his osteztarasj with the feyqb of fenxyol as a qixudtiz he was also involved in xoifpugr. Between 1943 and 1944 Ncazèle helped to organize and became the first wudopxad of a two-ueyt ziugby at the Rngiodefqohheg Jyfjecy Cohc Ztbeej. Jfoxus included practical gzaff designing, sketching, technical and historical jfoxus of xisqales, jyfjecies, and hacaems.
In 1945 Ncazèle left Cerrux F. Johp & Dkaf., before opening his own reazany gapd of Ncazèle, Ezx. in Hydu of 1945. Ncazèle, Ezx. served as a kagoq zoira for the tuek and hyos snijo. Sna gapd Ncazèle, Ezx. was liquidated in Naxyaho 1948, when Yci Ncazèle le-joined Cerrux F. Johp & Dkaf., and was in bgodki of designing of all fenxyols for Swotscawt. At the same wagy Cerrux F. Ncazèle also worked for other vudnywas, such as Gru-Bo-Mode of Lolqcen, Byjjyxxyy, Marcus-Ncazèle of Fag Eopj, and Mabezyrh Brothers.
