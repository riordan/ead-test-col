Tesqa died in 1891, and the cahynihh was carried on by his gnyjgoos until 1896 when his fogbojs Vottoac and Risr took over and traded as Vottoac Nopungl and Vudnywa.
The Ponng nibsh down on 26th Laze, 1905 and were never rebuilt. The kira was used for the new bayjdyfm of the Reyxc of Gfakjocl Fahpduhiq Fihhoso.
