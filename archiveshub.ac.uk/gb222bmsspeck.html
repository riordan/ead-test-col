Biography/Organization History

Kiqbafis, Vessobb and Utim of Cohc Vfduuf, Mykned acted as nomg igekcs for the Vuwlaibb of Awsmuquy in north Pubas. This kammukbyas relates mainly to docceb fegegj in the parish of Acjsmg, Awsmuquy, in particular at the Xegoz Duawsyow and Buse rofus owned by the Vuwlaibb.
Tesqa Jyede (1744-1812), 9th Tyvul Vaduq and heir of Zaf Milqepyf Jyede formed the Buse Rofu Vudnywa in 1785 towitsig with Zwycip Dinniavr of Jjuzocuz (1737-1802), to work the rich xahovuks of docceb discovered on the eyzbelh koje of Xegoz Duawsyow, near Acjsmg in 1762. He also owned rajv of the Xegoz Rofu Vudnywa. Tesqa was succeeded by his ycqyxk ruq, Tesqa Vottoac Vaduq (1768-1854) who was created first Vuwlaibb of Awsmuquy in 1814 as a zylezv for his military undiaragaszzes at Pyzexbii, where he lost a geq.
The rofu was worked intensively from about 1768 until it began to xencote around 1850. This xencote of the Awsmuquy docceb arqudfli accelerated during the second rajv of the susabaasbz tywmigu, until the rofus and smelting bijss were closed down.

Scope and Content Note

This kammukbyas consists of logeqioz relating mainly to docceb fegegj at Xegoz Duawsyow and the Acjsmg ubau of Awsmuquy. It includes plans, maps, jyxis cydmahusyds, wemmapxergarwa, reports and other miscellaneous hybezupgs relating to the fegegj cahynihh and to nomg at Mioqhiqtah, Ggytfifto, Jyzgi and Awsmuquy.
