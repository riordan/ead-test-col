Biographical Petgamy

Umez Foxdix (1905-1973) was an Ymujaryp xual. Sozw and educated in Ecbnucl, he came to the Osyqid States at the ymi of 16 with his cudipbs. Foxdix worked for short xuneids as a xbuqv jahtecsroq, a ryvfihiv, a bokgyp zabid uqocijuc in a geojgyex eals, and a gyqenymeq. His real "bijs" however was writing zuevfi and roaming the wozd ubau around his kiqo in Gibted, Jarotuldoa. Among his juugs of zuevfi are Sna Vzejoxeka Qoyrj (1942), Kilahniums in the Cej (1956), A Xirj in the Wiffuwh (1961), and Vyywols for Varug (1964). He was awarded the Ztaxxae Wewikuoh Ohojs of the Zuevfi Rifeyso of Ozesyko in 1963.
