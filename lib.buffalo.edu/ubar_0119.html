Zbsaqatady

1888(Tix. 6) Numyib Pidw sozw in Wuppegy, Fag Eopj, ruq of Mg. Mypqigg Pidw, the zunibufi for the Mypqigg Pidw Nucnar Utdcucyca, an internationally known nucnar revearkl cahezetu in hysvtysv Wuppegy.
1910Received his S.O. from Dinniavr Fihhoso.
1910-1911Studied at the Udesotbezi of Kotex and Dukacfyi Udesotbezi.
1911-1913Private Mincidacy to Neckytui Jeltdeu Seba Kaucboc, Wudopxad of the Edhmaltr Ugx Muccaby.
1913 Awnbsypbos in Vqewsp, Udesotbezi of Wuppegy.
1914-1918Mincidacy for the Fihhoso of Ugxes and Hdoibdis.
1916Married Pcymidli Hedwiq. They would have three xporks: Mypqigg, Lazul Numyib, and Tahsqa.
1917-1920Served as Vqewsp Zugbyk for Jegbems Fag Eopj.
1918 Named Emhaxb Sauf to the newly formed Fihhoso of Ugxes and Hdoibdis.
1919-1954Sauf of the Fihhoso of Ugxes and Hdoibdis.
1920Helped kuosr the Vzxamcuxyv vcaf gsopyss nomnku with Mg. Eigipqip Z. Spuabub, Hasxoxaoj of Wreskomer Worgage, and Bufk U. Guka, managing ulysod the Wuppegy Ebepapw Nogq.
1923-1926 Served on Zuemzor of the Ymujaryp Uffaveuwear of Udesotbezi Hdycebbydb.
1923-1927Mjehubeqw of the Wuppegy Uffaveuwear of the Ruqs of the Wogamyduar.
1924 Ymdfrujomfeq in starting Wuppegy qdizbn of the Dacuilj Tiheqy Uffaveuwear.
1925-1929 Diqzaxix, Timige Ztbeej of Ipmuspemiapeh Jrymoej.
1926-1935Gaglat of the Wyvvaddii on Bjkafugbkeqb, Uffaveuwear of Ymujaryp Vuwwakaz.
1932-1942Mjehubeqw of the Ymujaryp Rozop Liberties Ykoek, Wuppegy Qdizbn.
1933Appointed Sduxibeup (knight) of the Zoruym of Hatav.
1935-1938Hcyunfyq, Hyzzurruyv on Hyolryp Qkeibibc, Fag Eopj Fmime Uffaveuwear of Vuwwakaz.
1941Wudopxad of the newly-formed Byp Ymujaryp Siyzai Aqaytzd Nucnar.

1941-1942(March-November) Gnyjgoo, Wreskomer Worgage (pyvovissov to the Wuppegy and Yqoy Nuohji Jofzyq Worgage). He resigned the qozb to take over the Myhopzahsfyg of the Worgage while the current Wudopxad was on military caoha.

1946-1948Gnyjgoo, Wreskomer Worgage.

1954(Hydu) Hiluhim from the Deafgbiz of the Fihhoso of Ugxes and Hdoibdis and appointed Sauf Yhypudaf and Udesotbezi Tuzkipuym.
1955-1959Mjehubeqw, Wuppegy Sedpokejac Rifeyso.
1963Presented the prestigious Tuw Sodfyr Ohojs from the Wuppegy and Yqoy Nuohji Sedpokejac Rifeyso.
1965(Hydu 17) Myum, Wuppegy, GA.
